About Me

Sebastian Quezada Rojas

Biotechnology Engineer. Data Scientist. MSc in Biotechnology. PhD in Neuroscience. Father. Husband. Mentor. MacGyver wannabe. Star Wars fan. Should be enough, right?

Hello! Nice of you to stop by. My name is Sebastian Quezada Rojas and I am a Biotechnology Engineer of Latin American descent with a passion for science and critical thinking, an obsession with data and innovation, and a knack to fix things around the house using leftover or discarded materials. I obtained a MSc in Biotechnology in Chile by designing a recombinant vaccine against Avian Pathogenic Escherichia coli, and I got my PhD here in Australia studying the genetics (RNA Seq) and microstructure (MRI) of the cortical plate of the fetal sheep cortex as it adopts its folding pattern. I have also worked in pulmonary research, cardiovascular research, and brain injury, repair and regeneration research. I have done a fair bit of researching around! In fact, it's been over 10 years of scientific career! And just like biological beings, scientific careers also evolve - it is now time to move onto the field of data science to embrace a brighter, data-driven future.

My mission is to deliver simple but effective and innovative solutions. This applies not only to data and science, but to life in general. I believe things should be efficient, logical and reproducible, and I go out of my way to make things happen within deadlines. I am continually learning new ways of doing things, new approaches to analyse data, and always doing so with an open mind - always searching for better ways to deliver results, insights and visualisations.

So, what do you have in mind? Is there something I can help you deliver? Or are you looking for someone to collaborate? Shoot me an email, or contact me via LinkedIn. You can use the icons at the top or in the navigation bar. I'm always here to help!
