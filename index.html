Differential Genetic Expression
in Fetal Sheep Brain

This is a replication and an extension of a bioinformatics project that I undertook for my PhD in Neuroscience with Monash University (Australia). Here, I analise the differential genetic expression of two regions of the developing cortical plate derived from bulk RNA Seq to better understand what drives folding of the cerebral cortex.
See doi: 10.1093/cercor/bhaa171 for the full published study.
